Jumbotron

Component

Sam St Aubyn

Welcome to my website, come in and have a look around!

Button

Usage

  1. This jumbotro is to be used on welome screens to invite users in.
  2. All objects on the jumbotron image are to be centered.

<div class="jumbotron">

Welcome to my website, come in and have a look around!<br>

</div>
</div>
